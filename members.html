BTS

BTS debuted on June 13, 2013 under BigHit Entertainment. Known as Bangtan Sonyeondan, they are a seven-member South Korean boy group blending vocals, rap, and dance. Some hits include “Dynamite”, “Butter”, and “Boy With Luv”. They are beloved worldwide for their artistry and connection with ARMYs.
