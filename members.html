Meet the Members of BTS 💜

💜 “Welcome to our BTS family — a space made with love, laughter, and purple hearts. Here, we celebrate seven men who taught us to dream, to love ourselves and to never give up. Through music, stories, and moments, we keep their message alive — because BTS and ARMY are one, forever.” 💜

As RM once said, ‘We’re not seven with you — we’re seven with you.’.

From “No More Dream” to “Yet to Come,” BTS have inspired millions through their music and love for ARMY. Their unity continues to shine no matter where life takes them.
